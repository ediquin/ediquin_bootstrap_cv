Conoce a Edilson Laura

I am an Electronics Engineer specialized in Computing Systems, with a strong passion for innovation and technology. With over a year of experience in the tech industry, I have worked on web development, electronic circuit design, and IoT-based systems.

Currently, I work as a PS Engineer at Code Road Inc., where I contribute to the development of automotive dealership websites and perform Quality Assurance (QA) analysis.

I have also developed backend systems using PHP and MySQL, deployed cloud applications, and worked on embedded systems.

My expertise includes frontend development with React and Bootstrap, backend development with PHP and MySQL, and programming in C/C++, Python. I am always eager to take on new challenges and keep learning about emerging technologies.
